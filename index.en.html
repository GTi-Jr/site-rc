A little about us

With the experience of those who work for 15 years in the market, RC Hidropneumática stands out for its services and the distribution of quality hydraulic and pneumatic materials. In addition, we have a team of technicians and engineers able to indetize and point out the best solutions for you and your company. Check below our mission, our vision and our values.

Mission

Attend with excellence to the needs of our customers offering the best solutions, products and services, guaranteeing their satisfaction.

Vision

To be the most profitable company of the hydraulic and pneumatic sectors in the next five years, possessing a high degree of commitment of its employees, and providing to our clients, products and services with total quality, guaranteeing their satisfaction, preference and fidelity.

Values

Hydraulic and pneumatic products and services for the industrial sector.
